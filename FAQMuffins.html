FAQ

Come check some frequently asked questions about us!



When did you start baking?

We started a few years back, not in a moment when we needed money, but in a moment of motivation.

Are the flavors final?

Of course not! We will continue making new flavors until we eventually close up.

What is the Schedule opening and closing times?

We provide service from 10 AM till 6 PM! However, we close for Sunday so our employees may go to church.

Where are your locations?

We have a location in each state save for US territories, however the last state we touched was Kentucky!

Are you currently hiring?

We are currently accepting job applications. We pay $18 an hour!

Where do you receive your ingredients from?

We enjoy getting our fresh brands straight from the grocery store, the moment they hit shelves, we purchase them and make us of them immediately!

Is there discount days?

We have discounts on our food on Wednesdays and Fridays, we also offer discounts on holidays!

Do you serve special muffins?

Of course! We serve special muffins on certain days like the "Morning Muffin" on Mondays and the "Sleepy Muffin" on Fridays!

Do you accept custom orders?

We certainly accept custom orders. We may add frosting to the Muffins, add dishes alongside the muffins and even drinks.
